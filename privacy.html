SleepBlend

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

SleepBlend is a sleep-sounds app made to do one thing: play relaxing audio all night. It is built to work entirely on your device, and this policy is short because there is genuinely nothing to disclose at length.

SleepBlend collects no data. None. No analytics, no advertising identifiers, no crash reporting, no accounts, no tracking of any kind. The app does not request the internet permission, which means it is technically incapable of sending anything off your device.

Data we collect

None. SleepBlend has:

  • No account or sign-in. We never ask for your name, email, or any identifier.
  • No analytics or telemetry. We do not know how you use the app, or that you use it at all.
  • No ads and no ad SDKs. Nothing in the app talks to an ad network.
  • No network access. The app does not declare the Android internet permission, so it cannot transmit data anywhere.

Data stored on your device only

To make its features work, SleepBlend saves some information locally on your phone, and nowhere else:

  • Your settings and saved sound mixes (volumes, timers, blends, preferences).
  • Imported audio files you choose to add, copied into the app's private storage.
  • Microphone recordings, if you use the "Record your own sound" feature. Recordings are made only while you have the recording screen open, are stored only in the app's private storage on your device, and are never uploaded, shared, or accessible to other apps. You can delete them at any time in Settings.
  • A usage streak (which calendar days you played a mix), used solely to show your streak counter. It never leaves the device.

Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all of the above.

Permissions we ask for, and why

PermissionWhy
MicrophoneOnly for the optional "Record your own sound" feature, only while you're recording. Never used in the background.
NotificationsShows the playback notification with pause/stop controls.
Exact alarmsPowers the optional nightly auto-start and gentle-wake alarm you schedule.
Battery exemptionRequested (optionally) so Android doesn't stop your sounds partway through the night — the app's core purpose.
Run after restartResumes your mix if your phone reboots during a sleep session, if one was playing.

You can decline the microphone and battery-optimization prompts and the rest of the app works normally.

Children

SleepBlend collects no data from anyone, including children. The app contains no ads, no links to social media, and no purchases beyond the one-time price paid in Google Play.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app ever changes any of the above (for example, if a feature required network access), this policy will be updated before that version ships, and the change will be called out in the app's update notes.
